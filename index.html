Image Loader Demo

A quick and dirty image loader demo.

Simply paste in a series of URLs, then push the "Update" button.

For example, try copying and pasting the following:

https://www.google.com/favicon.ico https://www.yahoo.com/favicon.ico https://www.bing.com/favicon.ico https://www.amazon.com/favicon.ico https://www.wikipedia.com/favicon.ico https://www.ebay.com/favicon.ico https://www.yandex.com/favicon.ico https://www.facebook.com/favicon.ico https://www.twitter.com/favicon.ico
